About Me

Welcome!

I am a software developer. I build full-stack applications that solve a wide variety of problems. Most of my work is in React/Redux, but I have extensive experience in server-side template pages and command-line Python programs as welll. Recent projects include a two-player web game based on the board game Blokus, a dispatch dashboard for pool maintenance companies, and an authentification routes package based on the Shopify login with a firebase backend.

I am also a Robotics Process Automation (RPA) developer, having used UiPath's RPA software to win their first ever North American hackathon with an events scraper that automatically pulled events with certain keywords from meetup.com and consolidated them in a single Google caldenar.
